ABOUT

Hey, welcome to StocksHack, your one point stop for all the details and data you need for your next step in the stock world. What StocksHack does is it provides a bunch of features, out of which the main one is future predictions for all the top Stock Exchanges accross the world. It does so by using an algorithm that was devised by using the data of all the 10 major stock exchanges from as early as 1990. So, the user just need to select their Stock Exchange and select the date for which they want the prediction, and hit submit and get the predictions on your fingertips. Next major feature of our website is that it provides the live points of all the 10 major exchanges and also show a 5-day movement graph to let the users understand the current market better. Then we also have a section for news and stuff related to Stock Market and investment. We've used news api for that. Hope you'll have a great time here!
